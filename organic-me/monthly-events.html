Take a look at our themed events!

Every month our cafe section runs themed events that correlate with the time of month. We runs events for a whole week and regularly update our website to show new events. During these events we may run special themed offers and introduce stock to our shop and cafe! Take a look at the some of the yearly events we run!

Christmas Event

Every year the cafe runs a christmas event. It is usually held in the cafe hall where a tuck shop is present, selling all ranges of organic christmas treats. Treats like mould wine, christmas cookies and sweets! We also have a christmas market where all our home-made wears are sold for people to enjoy and to ultimately bring in the christmas cheers when the time comes!
